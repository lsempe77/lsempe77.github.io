This report presents a comprehensive mapping review of evidence in Fragile and Conflict-Affected Settings (FCAS). Developed for the Research and Capabilities Consortium (RCC) under FCDO, it synthesizes research findings to inform policy and practice in challenging contexts.

An interactive tool for exploring the evidence is available at [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/spaces/lsempe77/fcas).
